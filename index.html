Tiny Swords

a quick, cute, and chaotic card game

What is it?

Tiny Swords is a tiny card game that you can take anywhere and play with two or more people. Each game consists of super fast duels or you can step it up and take on the other game modes! Each game is completely unique with all of the different tiny swords and tiny sword combinations offered in each box.

How to Play

The game is played 1 vs 1 and each player holds six cards: 3 duel cards and 3 sword cards. The goal is to remove all your opponents' sword cards from their hand. Each player will start off with Rock, Paper, Scissors using one of their duel cards. Whoever wins get to attack first! Be careful though. If the attacker finds the opponents' other duel cards, they get to counterattack and unveil one of your cards. Once a sword card is found, a new round will start. Last one without any of their sword cards loses! Fear not if you don't remember the rules, each box comes with a foldout and reversible manual for the entire game!

Modes

With the handy dandy game manual, it will also come with instructions on the other game modes you can tackle with all the Tiny Sword cards. This includes Tiny Swords Classic, Tag Team, Tournament, Mystery Draft, Duelist Kingdom, Tiny Swords Turbo, Rummy Swords, and Tiny Swords Conquest!

Tiny Swordfighters?

The tiny swordfighters are a band of misfits and weirdos! Each swordfighter has their own sword, a magical power that is unique, and a story behind them. They all seem like they fight out of spite but they're all just friends who goof around and fight over dumb stuff like who gets the last slice of pizza or clean up the place.

The mastermind behind it all!
